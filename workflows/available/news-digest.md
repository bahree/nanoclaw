# News Digest

Search for and deliver a curated news summary. Focus areas:

1. **AI and machine learning** - major announcements, paper releases, product launches
2. **Technology** - industry news, notable launches, security incidents
3. **Business** - market-moving events, notable earnings, deals

For each story:
- One-line headline
- One-line summary of why it matters
- Source name

Include 5-8 stories total. Skip anything trivial or clickbait. Prefer primary sources over aggregators.

Format: numbered list, grouped by category. Keep the whole digest under 400 words.
