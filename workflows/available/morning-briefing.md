# Morning Briefing

Deliver a concise daily briefing to start the day. Include:

1. **Weather** - Current conditions and forecast for today at the user's location
2. **Top news** - 3-5 headlines relevant to the user's interests (tech, AI, business)
3. **Calendar** - If email/calendar access is available, summarize today's meetings and events
4. **Priorities** - Based on recent conversations, remind about any pending items or follow-ups

Keep the briefing concise and scannable. Use bullet points, not paragraphs. Lead with the most actionable items.

Format for chat (WhatsApp-friendly):
- Use bold for section headers
- Keep each section to 2-3 lines
- Total length: under 500 words
